How do I sign up ?

It’s easy, simply subscribe to the plan that works best for you and we’ll get you onboarded right away.

How does this help me as a podcast creator?

PowerPod is for you if you want a signature style for your podcast. Apart from that, this is a service designed to save you the headache of dealing with massive file sizes during uploads on different platforms, Designing thumbnails, Drafting Descriptions, and most importantly, dealing with novice editors and keep going in circles over hours of footage.

Why don’t I just hire a freelancer?

Hiring freelancers can be a good option, if you like headaches. Finding good talent is hard or extremely expensive. We’ve done all the work for you, in an affordable and streamlined way.

When does my month start?

Your month starts from the date you officially sign up and engage our services.

How do I communicate feedback?

You’ll chat directly with your dedicated project manager on slack and use frame.io to drop reviews on the video

How will you post my videos?

You have the option to provide us access to your social media accounts, and we'll take care of scheduling and posting your videos across all platforms. Alternatively, if you prefer, we can send you the videos and you can post them yourself. Your convenience is our focus.
